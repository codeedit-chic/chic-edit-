Styling Advice

Style Tips

Practical, honest tips to help you dress better every day — no trends, no overwhelm.

01

Fit First, Always

A ₹500 shirt that fits well looks better than a ₹5000 one that doesn't. Get things tailored — it's cheaper than you think.

02

Neutrals as Your Base

Build your wardrobe in beige, white, black, and navy. Add colour through accessories — scarves, bags, earrings.

03

One Statement Piece Per Outfit

Let one item do the talking. A bold earring, a printed bag, a coloured shoe. Keep everything else quiet.

04

Invest in Shoes and Bags

People notice your shoes more than you think. One good pair of loafers or white sneakers elevates every outfit.
