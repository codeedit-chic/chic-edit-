White Shirt + Straight Jeans + Loafers = Timeless Style

This Week's Formula

The White Shirt Edit

A great outfit isn't about wearing more — it's about wearing the right things together. The white shirt formula is the foundation every wardrobe needs.

Why It Works

The Formula

White Shirt — crisp, slightly oversized
Straight Jeans — mid-rise, dark wash
Loafers — leather or suede, any neutral
Minimal bag — tote or structured shoulder

Why It Works

  • White reflects light upward — instantly brightens your face
  • Straight cut balances all body types without trying
  • Loafers add polish without the effort of heels
  • Works for college, office, brunch, or travel
  • Clean and versatile
  • Suitable for casual and smart-casual occasions
  • Easy to accessorize

Styling Principles

The Rules We Live By

Good style isn't expensive — it's intentional. These three principles will change how you get dressed.

Contact Us

01

Fit First, Always

A ₹500 shirt that fits well looks better than a ₹5000 one that doesn't. Tailoring is the most underrated style tool.

02

Neutrals as Base

Build your wardrobe in beige, white, black, and navy. Add colour through accessories — scarves, bags, earrings.

03

One Statement Piece

Let one item do the talking — a bold earring, a printed bag, a coloured shoe. Keep everything else quiet.
